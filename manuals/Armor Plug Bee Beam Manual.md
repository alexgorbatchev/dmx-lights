### 2. Product Appearance and Overview
This product is a stage lighting equipment characterized by its ability to change light color. Its use on the stage or entertainment venues allows for the rendering of atmosphere, highlighting of the central figure, creation of a sense of space and time, and composition of the performance space. It is an important part of the lighting design for a variety of occasions, such as: Bars, Performing Arts Bars, KTV, family entertainment venues, schools, hotels/theaters, auditoriums, conference halls, lecture halls, multi-function halls, studios, banquet halls, and music festivals.

### 3. Safety Information
* **3.1** This product is for adults only. Please keep it out of reach of children.
* **3.2** Please check whether the voltage meets the rated power and pay attention to the total circuit load before installation.
* **3.3** Please use this product indoors. Avoid humid, hot, or dusty environments. If there is water or other liquid in the product, cut off the power and contact the supplier.
* **3.4** Check the power cord and insulation sleeve. Avoid sharp objects.
* **3.5** Turn off the power before not using the lamps or cleaning them.
* **3.6** The light doesn't include spare parts for repair. Do not open or change the product.
* **3.7** If the product is in use and smoke is found, unplug it immediately. Contact the dealer.
* **3.8** Do not stare at the luminous body for a long time to avoid eye injury.

### 4. Installation and Operation Instructions
* **4.1** After the user opens the box, please check whether the model, specification and appearance of the lamps and lanterns are in accordance with, and check whether the lamps and lanterns fittings are complete, without mechanical damage, deformation, paint peeling, lampshade rupture, tilt of the light box and other phenomena.
* **4.2** The product power supply: AC100-240V 50/60HZ. To prevent electric shock, be sure to connect the protective ground. Lamps and lanterns should be fixed firmly and reliably, each fixed with no less than 2 screws or bolts. If the product is larger than 3KG, it should be fixed with pre-buried hooks or bolts. If steel tube is used as the bracket of the lamps, the inner diameter of the steel tube is generally not less than 10mm, and the wall thickness is not less than 15mm.

#### Installation Instructions:
1. Take out the Allen screws from the accessory package, attach the handle (bow frame), and fix it securely.
2. Light hooks are connected to handles and fixed. *Note: The light hook must be purchased separately.*

#### Control Panel Function:
Switch settings function through buttons:
* Press **MENU** button to switch the function.
* Press **UP/DOWN** button to switch the function or set the address code.
* After switching the function, press **ENTER** button to save the function.

This product adopts display mode operation and DMX mode operation, the specific settings are as follows:

### 6. Maintenance Matters
* **6.1** Lamps and lanterns into the scene should be placed neatly, firmly, and should pay attention to moisture. Handling should be light to avoid bumping lamps and lanterns, and preventing damage to the appearance or function of the equipment.
* **6.2** If the lamp has dust, smoke, or macro-particles, it will strip the appearance of the equipment, and even cause overheating damage to the equipment. Therefore, products need to be cleaned regularly to extend the service life of the light source.
  * Clean the fans and vents regularly to ensure the cooling function of the bulbs.
  * Clean the lens of the lamp regularly to maintain maximum brightness, or use an air gun to clean the surface from micro-particles.
* **6.3** Periodically check the appearance of lamps, the housing, whether the installation is firm, and whether the screws are all tightened to ensure extended operational lifespan and maximum brightness.
